Who is Kathryn?

Kathryn Barrows is a senior nursing student at Adventist University of Health Science. She is running for the 2nd Vice President position on the Florida Nursing Student's Association executive board. With a positive outlook, passion, and professionalism, Kathryn intends to bridge the gap between nursing students across the state and the resources available to the FSNA. She believes there is great strength and vulnerability in recognizing we all have some long-term tenderness for something that has lead us to chose Nursing as a profession. Kathryn wants to encourage nursing students to voice their passions to further promote the image of Florida Student Nursing Association by providing more resources and vendors at the 2017 convention that will help students grow as leaders
